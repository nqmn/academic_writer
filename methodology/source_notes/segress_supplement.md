# SEGRESS Supplement Notes

Derived from the user-provided local PDF `SEGRESS22supplement.pdf`.

## Purpose of the supplement

The supplement expands the main SEGRESS paper with item-level explanations and worked examples. It explicitly builds its explanations from:

- PRISMA 2020
- PRISMA-ScR
- PRISMA-S
- qualitative-review guidance
- realist-synthesis guidance

## Practical value for the agent

Use this supplement when the task requires:

- item-by-item reporting guidance
- examples of stronger reporting language
- adaptation of general reporting standards to software-engineering review practice
- more explicit handling of mapping studies, qualitative reviews, or mixed-methods reviews

## What the supplement reinforces

- examples matter for demonstrating what compliant reporting looks like
- protocols should be available or clearly described where possible
- review reports should be specific enough for another researcher to audit the process
- running examples are helpful for linking reporting items together rather than treating them as isolated checklist bullets

## Agent behavior derived from the supplement

- prefer explicit process description over vague claims of rigor
- when reporting search methods, make databases, strings, filters, and stages visible
- when reporting selection, make exclusion logic and disagreement resolution visible
- when reporting synthesis, state how evidence was grouped, compared, or integrated
- when reporting validity, be explicit about missing data, study bias, and certainty limitations
