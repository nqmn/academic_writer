# Kitchenham 2002 Preliminary Guidelines Notes

Derived from the user-provided local PDF `Kitchenham_2002_IEEETSE_721.pdf`.

## What this source contributes

This paper predates the later EBSE review guideline and focuses more broadly on improving empirical software-engineering research quality and reporting.

## Recoverable themes from the local source

- empirical software-engineering research quality is uneven
- methodological and statistical weaknesses can undermine conclusions
- explicit guidelines can support better design, conduct, and evaluation of empirical studies
- critical assessment should address design, data collection, analysis, and reporting

## How to use this source in the skill

Use it as background support when:

- critiquing empirical-study quality
- diagnosing methodological weaknesses in primary studies
- setting expectations for design, data collection, analysis, and reporting quality

Do not use it as the primary procedural source for modern SLR reporting. For that, prefer Kitchenham EBSE-2007-01, PRISMA 2020, and SEGRESS.
