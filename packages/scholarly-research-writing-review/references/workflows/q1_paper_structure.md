# Q1 Paper Structure and Templates

This file provides structural templates, formulas, and section-order conventions for Q1 WoS manuscripts. It is complementary to `q1_wos_readiness.md`, which covers quality standards, reviewer weights, and rejection risks.

---

## Canonical Section Order

```
Title
Abstract
Keywords
1. Introduction
2. Literature Review
3. Theoretical Framework and Hypotheses
4. Methodology
5. Results
6. Discussion
7. Implications
8. Conclusion
9. Limitations
10. Future Research
References
Appendices
```

---

## Title Formula

```
[Core Variable or Innovation] + [Method or Approach] + [Context or Impact]
```

Examples:

- "Explainable AI Framework for Sustainable Smart Manufacturing: A Deep Learning Perspective"
- "Blockchain Adoption and Supply Chain Resilience: Evidence from Emerging Economies"
- "Machine Learning-Based Predictive Healthcare Analytics for Early Disease Detection"

---

## Abstract Template

```
This study investigates [topic/problem] in the context of [industry/country/domain].
Despite growing attention to [topic], limited studies have explored [specific gap].

To address this gap, this study proposes/examines/develops [model/framework].
Using [methodology] with data collected from [sample/source], the study applies [analysis method].

The findings reveal that [main results].
Additionally, [secondary findings].

This study contributes to the literature by [theoretical contribution].
Practically, the findings provide insights for [industry/policy/managers].

The study advances understanding of [research area] and offers directions for future research.
```

---

## Problem Statement Formula

```
Although previous studies have investigated [topic],
limited attention has been given to [specific issue],
particularly in [context].

Existing findings remain inconsistent regarding [issue],
creating uncertainty in both theory and practice.

Therefore, a comprehensive investigation is needed to address [gap].
```

---

## Discussion Formula

```
The findings suggest that [result].
This supports/challenges previous studies by [authors].

One possible explanation is [reasoning].
The findings further extend [theory/framework] by demonstrating [contribution].
```

---

## Future Research Paragraph Template

```
Future studies may extend this work by employing longitudinal designs
to better capture causal relationships over time.

Cross-country comparative investigations may further improve
the generalizability of the findings.

Additionally, future research could integrate moderating variables
such as organizational culture and digital maturity.
```

## Reviewer Expectations by Section

| Section      | Reviewer main focus    |
|--------------|------------------------|
| Abstract     | Novelty and clarity    |
| Introduction | Research gap           |
| Literature   | Critical synthesis     |
| Methodology  | Rigor and validity     |
| Results      | Accuracy               |
| Discussion   | Contribution           |
| Conclusion   | Impact                 |
| Future Work  | Scholarly direction    |
