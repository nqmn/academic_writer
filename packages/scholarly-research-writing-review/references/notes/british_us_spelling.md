# British and US Spelling Notes

Adapted from the "A Note on British and US Spelling" section of the source PDF.

## Rule

- Select either British English or US English at the beginning of a manuscript and maintain it consistently.

## Common Patterns

- `analyse` / `analyze`
- `organisation` / `organization`
- `behaviour` / `behavior`
- `centre` / `center`
- `labour` / `labor`
- `programme` / `program` when referring to a course or formal programme

## Operational Rules

- Use British spelling for UK institutions and journals unless house style states otherwise.
- Use US spelling for US institutions and journals unless house style states otherwise.
- Do not mix variants inside a single manuscript except in direct quotations or official names.
