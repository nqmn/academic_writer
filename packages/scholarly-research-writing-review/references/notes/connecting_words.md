# Connecting Words Notes

Adapted from the "A List of Words and Phrases for Connecting Ideas" section of the source PDF.

## Functional Categories

- Addition: `also`, `moreover`, `in addition`, `furthermore`
- Adversativity: `yet`, `however`, `nevertheless`
- Clarification: `that is`, `in other words`
- Consequence: `thus`, `therefore`, `as a result`, `consequently`
- Contrast: `in contrast`, `unlike`, `while`, `whereas`
- Illustration: `for example`, `for instance`
- Reason: `due to`, `owing to`, `because of`, `since`, `because`
- Sequence: `firstly`, `secondly`, `finally`, `in conclusion`

## Operational Rules

- Choose transitions according to the logical relation, not habit.
- Prefer one clear transition over stacked markers.
- Distinguish sentence-level connectors from subordinators inside a clause.
