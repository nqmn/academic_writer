# Major Revision Response Pattern

## Behaviour

- Acknowledge the substance of the concern.
- Explain the revision in concrete terms.
- State whether new analysis, text, or limitation language was added.

## Template

"We thank the reviewer for raising this important concern. In response, we have revised the manuscript to [action]. Specifically, we have added [analysis/clarification/text] in [section/page/paragraph] and now discuss the limitation more explicitly."
