# Cadence Rules

## Required Variation

- Alternate short, medium, and longer sentences where appropriate.
- Avoid repeating the same sentence opener across adjacent sentences.
- Do not close every paragraph with the same type of summary claim.

## Common Failure Patterns

- Repeated "Moreover", "Furthermore", or "However" openings.
- Uniform sentence length across a paragraph.
- Predictable three-sentence structures repeated across sections.
