# Paragraph Structure Notes

Adapted from the "Paragraph Structure" section of the source PDF.

## Default Paragraph Logic

1. Introduce the paragraph topic.
2. Develop the point with evidence, explanation, or examples.
3. Interpret the significance where appropriate.
4. Transition to the next point without mechanical repetition.

## Operational Rules

- Keep one controlling idea per paragraph unless the genre requires more compression.
- Avoid paragraphs that merely list sources without synthesis.
- Place the most informative sentence early when the reader needs orientation quickly.
