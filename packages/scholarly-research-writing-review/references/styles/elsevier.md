# Elsevier Style Overlay

## Priorities

- Balance literature synthesis, methodological justification, and cautious interpretation.
- Maintain a formal but readable research narrative.
- Make contribution claims explicit but proportionate.

## Writing Behaviour

- Use clear paragraph progression from context to gap to contribution.
- Integrate citations into synthesis rather than listing studies mechanically.
- Explain why methodological choices suit the research question.

## Preferred Moves

- Contrast prior findings before introducing the niche.
- Discuss limitations as part of interpretive rigor.
- Emphasise how findings extend or refine current understanding.

## Avoid

- Overcompressed argumentation that obscures the logic.
- Unsupported novelty language.
- Excessive repetition of stock transition markers.
