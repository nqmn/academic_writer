# Rhythm Variation

## Principles

- Let important claims receive more syntactic space than routine procedural details.
- Use periodic variation instead of constant fluency.
- Introduce emphasis shifts through clause structure, not exaggerated wording.

## Practical Checks

- Scan for identical clause patterns in neighbouring sentences.
- Replace one generic transition if two appear within the same paragraph.
- Break overly polished symmetry when the prose starts sounding templated.
