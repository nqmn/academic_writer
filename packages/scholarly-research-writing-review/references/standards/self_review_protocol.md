# Self-Review Protocol

This module defines the internal review pass the agent should perform before returning substantial academic output.

## Purpose

The agent should not stop at producing plausible prose. It should inspect its own output for rigor, coherence, evidential adequacy, and publication readiness.

Apply this protocol by default to:

- manuscript sections
- full manuscript drafting
- literature reviews
- research-gap positioning
- reviewer responses
- protocol-style review outputs

## Core Rule

Before finalizing, the agent should ask:

1. Does the output actually answer the task?
2. Are the claims supported and proportionate?
3. Does the structure match the intended genre or section?
4. Does the writing meet the active quality standard?

If the answer to any of these is weak, revise before returning the output.

## General Review Pass

Check for:

- task alignment
- section alignment
- coherence across paragraphs
- internal consistency
- redundancy
- unsupported assertions
- overclaiming
- awkward or formulaic phrasing

## Q1-Focused Review Pass

Where the Q1 WoS standard is active, explicitly check:

- novelty is specific
- the gap is evidenced, not merely asserted
- the literature is synthesized rather than listed
- the theoretical or conceptual basis is visible where relevant
- the methodology is justified where relevant
- the discussion interprets rather than repeats
- the contribution is explicit and differentiated
- implications are not generic

## Section-Specific Review Checks

### Introduction

Check:

- importance is clear
- the gap is explicit
- novelty is stated
- contribution is stated
- objectives or questions are clear

### Literature review

Check:

- studies are grouped analytically
- comparisons are real, not cosmetic
- contradictions or tensions are acknowledged
- the review leads toward a research need

### Methods

Check:

- design is named
- rationale is given
- key procedures are transparent
- validity, reliability, or trustworthiness are addressed when relevant

### Results

Check:

- results match the stated objectives or hypotheses
- the reporting is clear
- interpretation does not intrude too early

### Discussion

Check:

- findings are interpreted
- theory or prior literature is engaged
- contradictions are explained when relevant
- implications and limitations are specific

### Conclusion

Check:

- the conclusion answers the objective
- the contribution is restated clearly
- limitations and future work are proportionate

## Literature-Synthesis Review Checks

Check:

- the synthesis is thematic rather than sequential
- claimed gaps arise from the body of evidence
- disagreements are not flattened
- the positioning logic is explicit

## Reviewer-Response Review Checks

Check:

- each response addresses the actual reviewer concern
- revisions are described concretely
- tone is professional and non-defensive
- no generic filler replaces a substantive reply

## Anti-AI Review Checks

Check:

- repeated sentence openings
- transition stacking
- prestige-word inflation
- generic conclusions
- empty significance language
- mechanical use of phrasebank patterns

## Output Rule

Do not expose this self-review process unless the user asks for it.

Use it internally to improve the returned output.
