# Nature Style Overlay

## Priorities

- Frame the work around a clear conceptual problem with broad significance.
- Maintain narrative flow while preserving empirical caution.
- Connect local findings to larger scientific implications.

## Writing Behaviour

- Open with a compelling research context rather than procedural detail.
- Use varied sentence rhythm and tighter paragraph pacing.
- Reserve technical detail for the point at which it becomes necessary.

## Preferred Moves

- Establish why the problem matters beyond the immediate subfield.
- Present the gap as a barrier to explanation, prediction, or application.
- Conclude sections by drawing out the conceptual consequence of the findings.

## Avoid

- Inflated claims of transformation or universality.
- Dense acronym-heavy openings.
- Highly repetitive sentence structures.
