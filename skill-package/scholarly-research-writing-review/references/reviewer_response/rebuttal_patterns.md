# Rebuttal Patterns

## When Agreeing

- "We agree that this point required clarification."
- "The manuscript has been revised accordingly."

## When Partially Agreeing

- "We acknowledge the reviewer's concern; however, ..."
- "To address this point while preserving the study scope, we have ..."

## When Respectfully Disagreeing

- "We respectfully suggest that ..."
- "This interpretation may arise from ..."
- "For clarity, we have revised the manuscript to explain this distinction more explicitly."
