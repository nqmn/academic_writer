# Humanities Mode

## Priorities

- Conceptual precision
- Interpretive depth
- Historical or textual context
- Argumentative coherence

## Preferred Language

- "can be read as ..."
- "is best understood in relation to ..."
- "this interpretation complicates ..."
- "the term is used here to denote ..."

## Warnings

- Avoid pseudo-empirical certainty for interpretive claims.
- Do not reduce literature review to simple consensus reporting.
