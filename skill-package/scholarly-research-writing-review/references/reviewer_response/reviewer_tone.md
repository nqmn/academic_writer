# Reviewer Tone

## Requirements

- Remain professional and specific.
- Avoid defensive or emotional phrasing.
- Emphasise revision, clarification, and evidence.

## Avoid

- "The reviewer misunderstood ..."
- "Clearly ..."
- "As already stated ..."

## Prefer

- "We have clarified ..."
- "We acknowledge this limitation ..."
- "To make this point clearer, we have added ..."
