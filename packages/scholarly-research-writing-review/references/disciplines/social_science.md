# Social Science Mode

## Priorities

- Interpretive nuance
- Theoretical framing
- Context sensitivity
- Reflexive methodological explanation

## Preferred Language

- "within this context ..."
- "these findings suggest ..."
- "is shaped by ..."
- "may reflect broader structural factors ..."

## Warnings

- Avoid overgeneralising beyond the study setting.
- Keep theory linked to observed evidence.
