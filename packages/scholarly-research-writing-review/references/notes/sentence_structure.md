# Sentence Structure Notes

Adapted from the "Sentence Structure" section of the source PDF.

## Requirements

- Use complete clauses.
- Ensure dependent clauses are not left standing as sentences.
- Join independent clauses correctly with conjunctions or punctuation.
- Vary sentence length while preserving clarity.

## Operational Rules

- Check for sentence fragments after revision.
- Check for comma splices when two independent clauses appear together.
- Use subordination when showing contrast, cause, concession, or condition.
- Prefer clarity over ornate complexity.
