# Contribution Framing

## What to Emphasise

- What the study adds
- Which gap it addresses
- How the addition changes understanding, method, or practice

## Framing Patterns

- "This study contributes to the literature by ..."
- "The findings extend current understanding of ..."
- "The analysis provides new evidence regarding ..."
- "The study offers a framework for ..."

## Constraint

- Contributions must remain proportional to the evidence actually presented.
