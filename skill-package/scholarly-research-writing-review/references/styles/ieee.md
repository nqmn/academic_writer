# IEEE Style Overlay

## Priorities

- Maximise information density without sacrificing readability.
- Prefer direct topic sentences and short transitions.
- Keep novelty claims specific and technically bounded.

## Writing Behaviour

- Use concise sentences and low narrative expansion.
- Report methods and results with strong procedural clarity.
- Prefer quantified statements over evaluative adjectives.

## Preferred Moves

- State the technical problem early.
- Position the contribution against concrete system or method limitations.
- Summarise outcomes with performance-oriented wording.

## Avoid

- Long contextual build-up before the research problem.
- Broad claims of impact without measured support.
- Decorative transitions or literary framing.
