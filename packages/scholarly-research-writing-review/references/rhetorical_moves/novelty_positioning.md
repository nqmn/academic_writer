# Novelty Positioning

## Contribution Dimensions

- Methodological novelty
- Architectural or system novelty
- Dataset or corpus novelty
- Analytical novelty
- Theoretical novelty
- Performance novelty
- Integration novelty

## Positioning Rules

- Compare the contribution against a concrete baseline in prior work.
- Use modest formulations such as "extends", "refines", "provides new evidence", or "offers a more explicit account".
- Separate novelty from importance; a study may be valuable without being radically unprecedented.

## Avoid

- "For the first time" unless strictly defensible.
- Inflated claims of disruption or transformation.
- Vague novelty language detached from the actual contribution.
